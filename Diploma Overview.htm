Diploma Overview and Pedagogy

Overview

In today’s world, there are several Certification Programs available that ensure mastering of skills and knowledge to excel in technical, managerial and administrative perspectives. There are very few programs that cater towards philosophical and spiritual foundations and among these, to the best of our knowledge, there might not be such certification curricula that correlate natural and acquired skills based on spiritual and philosophical foundations. To mention a few, positive thinking, stress management, ethics and values, universal brotherhood are important markers to script success stories of a person in the professional world. Along with aptitude, the attitude of a person to handle circumstances and situations is very important and programming efforts based on a richer combination of spiritual and emotional quotients based on academic endeavors is missing in the contemporary educational system.  Personality traits are often taken granted and hence attitude is not stressed upon that has a stronger dependence on the spiritual dimensions being cultivated by a person and a system. Thus, spiritual foundation has a unique and special role to promote these skills.  Other than these, few other skills are relevant as well for a person to earn accolades in personal, professional and social spheres of life. These include analytical/creative aptitude, self-control and focus, communication skills and awareness. In summary, the thrust or thematic emphasis of the Certification Program (Diploma) is to nurture subjective excellence and thereby achieve distinction in the fulfilment of objectives/goals in life.

The Certification Program (Diploma) in Science-Spirituality is a carefully designed flagship program of Bhaktivedanta Institute by its Distance Education team. Unlike Certificate courses, the Certification Program attempts to target the renaissance of subjective proficiencies of a delegate. This is achieved through the imparting of philosophical skills and spiritual foundations of life. The Diploma primarily targets (a) the inculcation of strong philosophical and spiritual foundations based on the Bhaktivedanta school of thought and (b) application of philosophical and spiritual foundations for excellence and distinction in human life in five major themes namely God, Management, Research, Health and Philosophy. Also, a dissertation component has been included in the Diploma to enhance realizations on the universality of science-spirituality synthesis vision of Dr. T. D. Singh. Considering these aspects, the Certification Program (Diploma) is expected to have an edge and uniqueness in comparison with many other such existing programs.

Pedagogy

Modern education excessively involves empirical concepts and methodologies. Due to such an approach, subjective components including holistic skill development, ultimate meaning and purpose of life, philosophical and spiritual foundations are often ignored. Hence, with a significant thrust on only objective but not subjective pedagogy, modern education cannot be regarded to be complete. The after effect of such gross imbalance in the educational system is evident. Spirituality driven holistic skill development, ethics and values, spirituality based social harmony, moral education based on the synthesis of world religions, holistic management, incessant positive thinking, spirituality based creative and analytical leaps, holistic stress management at work, scientific, philosophical and spiritual awareness are all highly relevant for holistic evolution of a person. However, these are few important elements that are not stressed in modern education.  It is a fact that these have profound relevance in scripting success stories in personal, professional and social spheres of life.

Advocating upon a foot print that is grossly rooted in philosophical and spiritual foundation, the Certification Program (Diploma) in science-spirituality attempts to equip delegates with fundamentals in holistic education. The Certification Program (Diploma) is based on the unique science-spirituality synthesis vision of Dr. T. D. Singh (His Holiness Bhaktisvarupa Damodara Swami, founding director of Bhaktivedanta Institute). Being holistic, the synthesis model of Dr. T. D. Singh is universal and can be applied to any field of human pursuit and excellence. Unlike many other science-spirituality educational programs, the Certification Program (Diploma) involves a balanced approach of science, philosophy and spirituality in the Bhaktivedanta tradition. Hence, the Certification Program (Diploma) is the most practical perception of reality based on the synthesis or integration of one’s own perception, perception of profound thinkers and perception based on the absolute or top-down approach. This is the need of the hour, given the fact that science and technology based human activity cannot be omitted but supplemented with philosophical and spiritual wisdom. In summary, science-spirituality based synthesis is highly contextual in the age of science and technology to progressively integrate subjective or personality factors into the scientific and technological domain.

The nature of Absolute Truth and its relevance to provide solutions for fundamental issues and problems of the modern man will remain a central theme in the Certification Program (Diploma). Therefore, the ultimate objective of the Certification Program (Diploma) in science-spirituality is to achieve distinction and excellence in human life.  After completing the Certification Program (Diploma), delegates are expected to have enriched combinations of emotional and spiritual quotient (EQ and SQ), and thereby become role models for a peaceful, prosperous and purposeful society.
